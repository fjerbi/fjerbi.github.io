In the framework of a partnership project aimed at enhancing skills in the field of green engineering and sustainable management, a meeting took place involving the National School of Engineers of Tunis (University of Tunis El Manar), the West Saxon University in Zwickau, Germany, the Engineering Training Office at the Tunisian Engineers Order, the Tunisian-German Friendship Association, and the Research and Innovation Development Association. The project also enjoys the support of the German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD) and aims to develop and implement innovation in educational programs at both the industrial and social levels.

In this context, the Dean of Tunisian Engineers received a delegation from the German university, the University of Tunis El Manar, the Tunisian-German Friendship Association, and the Research and Innovation Development Association on Thursday, December 12, 2024, at the Tunisian Engineers Order. I participated in this event as a research assistant with WHZ.

The meeting was dedicated to introducing the tasks of the Order, discussing ways of cooperation to activate mechanisms for integrating sustainable development goals in engineering education and continuous training programs, and enhancing the connection between the academic and professional environments to achieve a comprehensive vision of sustainable development.
